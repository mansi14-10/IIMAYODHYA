About Us

Our Mission: To Provide the Best Education.

The Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM) has approved a new branch in Ayodhya, the city of Lord Ram, to support the "Ek Bharat Shresth Bharat" initiative. The inauguration will be an illustrious event featuring prominent figures from the healthcare industry, academic institutions, hotel and hospitality sectors, and other industries, along with IIMM dignitaries.

Our Story

After getting associated with Indian Institute of Material Management (IIMM), I got the opportunity not only to improve my academic qualifications in Material Management (GDMM, PDMM & MBA (MM)) but also served IIMM Raebareli Branch in various capacities including Chairman of the Branch. The credit goes to our present National President Shri Lalit Raj Meena Sa who introduced me to IIMM during my service at SGPGIMS, Lucknow. During 2019-21 I was the only official who got elected unopposed to the Apex Body of IIMM known as National Executive Committee (NEC) in the capacity of Vice-President (North).
